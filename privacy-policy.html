Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

1. Introduction

LilithPlay ("we", "our", "us") is an interactive game app designed for couples. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Player names (stored locally on your device)
  • Profile photos/avatars (stored locally on your device)

Information from Google Sign-In (optional):

  • Google account email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL

Automatically collected:

  • Game preferences (language, music settings)
  • Game scores and progress (stored locally)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your account data across devices (if you sign in with Google)
  • To manage premium subscriptions

4. Data Storage

Local storage: Player names, avatars, game preferences, and scores are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device unless you sign in with Google.

Cloud storage: If you choose to sign in with Google, your account information is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only used within the app and stored on Google Firebase infrastructure when you opt in to Google Sign-In.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and cloud data storage
  • Google Play Billing — Subscription payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

7. Children's Privacy

LilithPlay is not intended for children under the age of 16. Our app includes an age verification gate. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16 years of age.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Local data is retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Cloud data (for signed-in users) is retained until you request account deletion. To delete your account, visit lilithplay.com/delete-account.

9. Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We use Google Firebase's security infrastructure to protect your cloud data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or our website.
