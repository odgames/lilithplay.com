Delete Your Account

If you'd like to delete your LilithPlay account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

HOW TO REQUEST DELETION

Send an email to the following address with the subject line "Delete My Account":

odgameinfo@gmail.com

Please send the request from the same email address associated with your Google Sign-In so we can verify your identity.

DATA THAT WILL BE DELETED

  • Google account link (email, display name, profile photo)
  • Firebase user record and authentication data
  • Firestore user document (premium status, login history)

DATA STORED ONLY ON YOUR DEVICE

  • Player names and avatars
  • Game preferences (language, music settings)
  • Game scores and progress

This data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. To remove it, uninstall the app or clear the app data from your device settings.

PROCESSING TIME

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
